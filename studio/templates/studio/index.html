About

Why I Build This Way

I believe in software that respects your time, your privacy, and your wallet. Too many tools today are designed to lock you into subscriptions, harvest your data, or create dependencies on cloud services you don't control.

The products in this studio are built differently. Where possible, they work offline. They don't require accounts. They don't phone home. You buy them once, you own them forever, and your data stays on your device.

This is indie software built by one developer who actually uses these tools every day. No venture capital, no growth hacking, no dark patterns — just honest tools that do their job well.
